Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED · MAY 9, 2026

The short version. NoJetLag does not require an account. Your flight details, sleep schedule, and feedback are stored only on your iPhone. The day-by-day plan is computed locally. The only third party that briefly touches data is Adapty, our paywall provider, which receives an anonymous device identifier — never linked to who you are.

1. What we collect

We do not collect personal data on our servers. Specifically:

  • Your flight details, bedtime, wake time, and trip feedback are stored only on your device, in app storage (a JSON file in the Documents directory).
  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or Apple ID.
  • We do not collect health data, location, contacts, calendar, microphone, or camera input.

2. On-device computation

The day-by-day light, sleep, and melatonin schedule is computed entirely on your iPhone from the flight you enter. No flight data, no sleep schedule, no feedback is transmitted to our servers or to any third party.

3. Third-party services

NoJetLag relies on one third party to deliver the first-launch onboarding screens and to manage subscription state:

  • Adapty (onboarding flow and subscription management) — https://adapty.io/privacy/
    Adapty receives an anonymous device identifier and onboarding-event analytics in order to render the onboarding screens and manage subscription state. Adapty does not receive your trip details, your sleep schedule, or any health-relevant data. The first launch requires an internet connection so Adapty can deliver the onboarding; after that, the app works fully offline.

4. What we do not do

  • No accounts, no sign-up, no email, no password.
  • No advertising, no ad networks, no ad-attribution SDKs.
  • No general-purpose analytics or telemetry. The Adapty SDK is configured for paywall and onboarding events only.
  • No selling, sharing, or licensing of any user data to anyone, ever.

5. Children's privacy

NoJetLag is not intended for users under 12 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If a parent or guardian believes that a child has used NoJetLag in a way they object to, please contact us at boytik@actvox.dev and we will respond as quickly as we can.

6. Your rights

Because we do not associate any data with an identifiable person, there is no personal account on our side to access, export, or delete. The data the app stores about you lives on your device, and you control it directly:

  • To clear your trip and start over, use the in-app Settings → Trip → Clear current trip action.
  • To wipe everything, uninstall NoJetLag from your iPhone — local data is removed when the app is removed.

7. Security

All network traffic between NoJetLag and Adapty is protected by standard TLS (HTTPS). We do not implement custom cryptography, and the app declares ITSAppUsesNonExemptEncryption = false in its Info.plist.

8. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page and, if the change is material, we will mention it inside the app. Continuing to use NoJetLag after a change means you accept the updated policy.

9. Disclaimer

NoJetLag is general guidance based on circadian-rhythm research. It is not medical advice and not a medical device. Recommendations do not constitute medical, legal, financial, or psychological advice. Melatonin availability and legal dose vary by country — consult a qualified healthcare professional before starting any melatonin protocol, particularly if you take prescription medication, are pregnant or breastfeeding, are under 18, or have a sleep, mood, or autoimmune condition.

10. Contact

Questions, concerns, or just want to say hi? Email boytik@actvox.dev. We read everything.
